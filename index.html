All Delightful Natural Food Teas are Hand-crafted (processed, produced, labored, and packaged) by Joe & Bobby Inc., a FDA registered food manufacturer in Modesto, CA.

In 2011, the company was incorporated as Joe & Bobby Inc. Joe is Delightful Natural Food’s Chief of Operations, and Bobby (Ma's eldest grandson) are the next two generations following Ma. Dà jiě (Ma's eldest daughter) together with other family members: Tom (Ma's son), Phillip and Annie (Ma's grandchildren), Yen (Ma's daughter), and Alain (Yen's husband) are able to pass Ma's legacy to future generations. We are committed to carrying on Ma's mission to help people be healthy and to produce Ma’s products that will allow them to do so. We hope you will join us in continuing Ma’s legacy.
